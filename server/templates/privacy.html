Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Self-hosted instances

If you self-host labwatch, your data never leaves your network. The labwatch server makes zero outbound connections except to notification channels you explicitly configure (Discord, Slack, ntfy, etc.). There is no telemetry, no analytics, no phone-home. We have no access to your data.

Managed service (labwatch.dev)

When you use the managed service at labwatch.dev, we collect and store:

  • Account data: your email address (for login and billing).
  • Infrastructure metrics: CPU, memory, disk, network, container, GPU, and S.M.A.R.T. data sent by the agent running on your nodes.
  • Alert history: alerts generated by threshold rules you configure.

We do not collect:

  • File contents, logs, or command output from your servers
  • Network traffic contents or DNS queries
  • Personal files, browsing history, or keystrokes
  • Any data beyond system-level metrics

Data storage & location

Managed service data is stored on infrastructure in Switzerland. Metrics are retained according to your plan's retention window (30 days for free, 1 year for paid plans). When you delete a node or cancel your account, associated data is permanently deleted.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or provide your data to any third party. We do not use your data for model training, advertising, or analytics. The natural-language query engine runs entirely on the server — no data is sent to any external AI/LLM provider.

Cookies

labwatch uses a single session cookie (labwatch_session) for authentication and optionally a language preference cookie (labwatch_lang). No analytics cookies. No third-party tracking scripts on any page.

Third-party services

  • Stripe: payment processing for paid plans. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Resend: transactional email delivery (signup confirmation, billing receipts).

No other third-party services receive your data.

Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Export all your data via the dashboard export feature (JSON).
  • Delete individual nodes or your entire account.
  • Request a full data export or deletion by emailing support@labwatch.dev.

For GDPR requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability), contact support@labwatch.dev. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@labwatch.dev.
